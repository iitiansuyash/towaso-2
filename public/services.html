Our Services

TowaSo is dedicated to making sustainable living a normalcy through scientific waste management solutions and spreading awareness among people about pollution management practices.

Consultancy

We provide consultation to municipal corporations about the most efficient waste management methods. Our team works round the clock to educate people.

Biogas

We use the organic matter derived from daily wastes to produce biogas, a highly useful and cost-effective fuel. The whole process is conventional and causes zero pollution.

IT solutions

All the methods opted by TowaSo for waste and pollution management are conventional and scientifically proven ways to prevent the wastage of any potent resource

Reduce Reuse Recycle

TowaSo stands strongly on the principle of 3Rs. We ensure that no potential resource gets wasted and is turned into a utile form beneficial to us also we conserve natural resources.

Waste management

The team of TowaSo works on the corporate and household levels to assist in proper waste disposal and utilization. We inform people about the ways to effectively waste management without adding up to pollution.

Vermi compost

We stand proudly to assist our clients in keeping our cities clean. Waste collection to segregation, followed by its utilization and management — we provide the best services to ensure utmost efficiency.
