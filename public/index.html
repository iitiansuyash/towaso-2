about towaso

TowaSo is an exceptional and ever-growing organization earnestly focused on the sustainable utilization of daily waste material and raising awareness about the ethical practices of waste management.

TowaSo was founded by two environmental enthusiasts, Saurabh Kumar (Founder and CEO), and Soni Kumari (Co-Founder and COO), who envisioned a world where the survival and development of humankind wouldn't involve harming nature and exploiting valuable potential resources.

The journey of TowaSo started with assisting end-to-end municipal waste management and creating awareness among people about the ethical methods of disposal and utilization of different types of waste products. Currently providing utilization solutions in Bihar and Jharkhand, we are committed to playing our part toward a sustainable world through waste management, resource management, and pollution management.

We follow the scientifically affirmed methods of waste collection, segregation, and utilization. A few of our many services involve composting, biogas generation, recycling, and production of environment-friendly daily-use products. The by-products derived from our waste processing practices are utile resources like organic vermin-fertilizers, precious metals, biogas, recycled paper, and plastic. In its 6 years, TowaSo has extended its services to cities and provided waste management counseling to over 1300 households and 3 schools.

Our Services

TowaSo is dedicated to making sustainable living a normalcy through scientific waste management solutions and spreading awareness among people about pollution management practices.

Consultancy

We provide consultation to municipal corporations about the most efficient waste management methods. Our team works round the clock to educate people.

Biogas

We use the organic matter derived from daily wastes to produce biogas, a highly useful and cost-effective fuel. The whole process is conventional and causes zero pollution.

IT solutions

All the methods opted by TowaSo for waste and pollution management are conventional and scientifically proven ways to prevent the wastage of any potent resource.

Reduce Reuse Recycle

TowaSo stands strongly on the principle of 3Rs. We ensure that no potential resource gets wasted and is turned into a utile form beneficial to us also we conserve natural resources.

Waste management

The team of TowaSo works on the corporate and household levels to assist in proper waste disposal and utilization. We inform people about the ways to effectively waste management without adding up to pollution.

Vermi compost

We stand proudly to assist our clients in keeping our cities clean. Waste collection to segregation, followed by its utilization and management — we provide the best services to ensure utmost efficiency.
